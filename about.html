About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Ogopogo Coatings has quickly established itself as a leader in the epoxy flooring industry. Named after the legendary lake creature of Okanagan Valley, our company embodies strength, resilience, and uniqueness in every project we undertake.

Our mission is to provide exceptional epoxy coating solutions that combine aesthetic appeal with unmatched durability. We serve residential and commercial clients throughout the region with a commitment to quality workmanship and customer satisfaction.

Our Approach

At Ogopogo Coatings, we use only premium materials and advanced application techniques to ensure your surfaces not only look stunning but also withstand the test of time. Our team of skilled professionals brings years of experience and dedication to every project, big or small.

Our Values

  • Quality without compromise - We never cut corners, using only the finest materials and most effective techniques.
  • Transparency and integrity - We provide honest assessments and fair pricing on every project.
  • Customer-focused service - Your satisfaction is our priority from initial consultation through project completion.
  • Environmental responsibility - We prioritize eco-friendly practices and products whenever possible.
